What is Toontown Fantasy about?

Toontown Fantasy is a singleplayer reinvention of the beloved Disney game, Disney's Toontown Online built those who've grown up with the original game and want something different! Create your own Toon and join the never-ending battle against the Cogs, who want to turn Toontown into their latest business venture.

The real Toontown Fantasy takes place outside the timeline you may know. The version I am referring to is of the Tooniverse was created by Aurora Lovelight in memory of her mother who she lost earlier in her timeline who always wanted to make that perfect Toontown game but probably never had the resources to do it!
